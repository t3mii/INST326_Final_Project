# Welcome to our INST326 farm simulator!! Below is the description, sources used, and gameplay loop.

## Game Description: 
We plan to create a turn based farming game where the player manages a small farm over a series of days. Each day the player makes choices such as planting crops, watering the fields, harvesting produce, buying seeds, and selling produce.  
The core gameplay loop involves taking daily actions, updating crop growth, and responding to random events such as weather changes or pests. These events affect crop growth and overall farm performance, so players must adjust their strategy as the game progresses. What makes the game interesting is that players must balance risk and reward, since poor planning or bad weather can lead to losses, while good decisions can increase profit and farm growth over time.
The game ends when the player enters "end game" to return their results since a player can play multiple farm cycles. The overall gist is to have the biggest farm within one game year.
## Gameplay Loop:
1. Player starts with a small plot of land and a certain amount of money
2. Each "day" the player makes decisions
3. Days pass and crops grow/events happen
4. Player tries to grow and expand their farm within one year
5. Game cycle ends at the end of the year and the game ends when the user enters “end game” to return their results
